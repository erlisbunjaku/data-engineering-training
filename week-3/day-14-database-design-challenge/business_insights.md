# Day 14 - Business Insights

## 37. Which program has the most active students?

The program with the most active students shows the highest current demand. Management should monitor resources and instructor workload for this program.

---

## 38. Which program generated the most collected revenue?

The program with the highest collected revenue is the strongest financial performer and can help guide future investments.

---

## 39. Which city has the most students?

The city with the most students shows where the training center has the strongest student base and marketing opportunities.

---

## 40. Which students are financially risky?

Students with unpaid or partial payments are financially risky and should be contacted to prevent revenue problems.

---

## 41. Which students are academically risky?

Students with low attendance rates may need additional support to improve progress and avoid dropping out.

---

## 42. Which program has the weakest attendance?

The program with the lowest attendance should be reviewed to understand possible issues with engagement or scheduling.

---

## 43. Which enrollments are missing payment records?

Enrollments without payments should be checked because they can create incomplete financial reports.

---

## 44. Which instructor has the highest number of active students?

The instructor with the most active students has the highest workload and may need additional support.

---

## 45. What should management focus on next?

Management should focus on improving attendance, following up on unpaid payments, supporting popular programs, and reducing student dropouts.

---

The database design made reporting easier because tables were connected with foreign keys and relationships.

The hardest part was designing relationships, especially connecting students and programs through enrollments.
