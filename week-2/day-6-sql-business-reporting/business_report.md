## 12. Orders that should not count as real revenue:

Pending and cancelled orders should not be counted as real revenue because they have not been successfully completed and the business has not received confirmed income from them.

## 13. One business recommendation:

## 14. One data quality or reporting risk:

Including pending or cancelled orders in revenue reports can overstate business performance and lead to incorrect decisions.
